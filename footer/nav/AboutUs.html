iORA Fashion first opened in 1998, committed to inspire and democratise quality through well sought-after apparels that will be worn and loved for a long time. iORA group offers an array of ready-to-wear pieces for women, from wardrobe staples to accessories that complete the look for all seasons and occasions.

iORA

iORA, a label for the contemporary working ladies. Create collections with great attention to details for modern-day women, reinventing classics that are timeless and meet the real considerations of a woman – always. Comfort and quality are deeply rooted in our DNA as we want women to feel unreservedly comfortable in their skin and carry themselves with confidence in iORA.
